What is MMIWG2S+?

MMIWG2S+ refers to Missing and Murdered Indigenous Women, Girls, Two-Spirit, Transgender, and Gender-Diverse people. The crisis affects Indigenous communities across North America.

“No data point is bigger than a life.”

Why complete lists are hard

  • Underreporting and inconsistent record-keeping
  • Racial misclassification and missing tribal affiliation
  • Jurisdictional gaps across tribal/state/federal/local systems
  • Fragmented databases and differing definitions

How this site uses data

Entries reflect publicly available sources. This is not exhaustive. The goal is education and remembrance—never sensationalism.

Methodology (short)

  • Cases are entered from publicly available sources and listed per card.
  • Some fields are unknown or not publicly released; those values remain blank or “Unknown.”
  • Language is factual and family-centered, avoiding speculation or graphic detail.
  • Removal requests are honored and changes are logged through repository history.
